Bed & Breakfast

All rooms have the following facilities:

  • En suite with power shower
  • Cable TV with 32" flatscreen TVs in most rooms
  • Luxury king size beds
  • Wifi
  • Private off-road parking for up to 12 cars
  • Tea and coffee available at any time

Our family rooms have a double and 2 single beds. Travel cot available on request.

Breakfast

We offer a continental breakfast, or a full Irish breakfast which is available on request while booking.

Breakfast times are from 6AM to 11AM, so you can have a lazy morning in bed or have breakfast before catching an early flight or ferry.

Apartments

Our self catering apartments are located just alongside the B&B and have the following facilites:

  • 2 bedrooms
  • Double and single bed in each bedroom
  • Modern fitted kitchen
  • Living room
  • Bathroom
  • Superb back gardens
  • Cable TV
  • Broadband Internet
  • Secure off road parking
  • Sleep up to 4 persons

They are ideal for a family or professionals coming to Cork for a period of a couple of weeks or months.
