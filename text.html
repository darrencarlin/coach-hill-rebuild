Modules

These are the textual modules available, you can place any of these within the containers you have already placed and they will stretch to fit the size of its container so either default, half or full.

There are several different options available from either using a heading on its own or with sub headings, paragraphs and even buttons



Headline - Main


		
		
			
			<div class="heading-1">
				<h1>Headline - Main</h1>
				<hr>
			</div>


Headline - Small

		
		
			
			<div class="heading-2">
				<h2>Headline - Small</h2>
			</div>
